# Skill: generate-brand

## Responsibility
Generate a brand identity definition including tagline, tone of voice, and color palette direction.

## Input
- `product_name`: Name of the product
- `concept`: One-sentence description of the product
- `audience`: Target audience

## Steps
1. Generate a one-line tagline.
2. Define tone of voice in 3 adjectives.
3. Suggest a color palette direction (2-3 colors with purpose).
4. Write a 2-sentence brand positioning statement.

## Output
- Tagline
- Tone of voice (3 adjectives)
- Color palette direction
- Brand positioning statement

## Notes
- Brand must align with the target audience's expectations.
- Keep the tone consistent across all generated assets.
